Reliance Industries shares hit record high

Positive 15 Nov 2025 5 min read
BS
Business Standard
Market News

Reliance Industries Ltd (RIL) shares surged to a record high on Friday, driven by positive investor sentiment after the company's annual general meeting. The stock jumped 5% to touch an all-time high of 2,343.90 on the BSE.

Mukesh Ambani, chairman of RIL, made several key announcements at the AGM, including plans to make Reliance a zero-net debt company within 18 months, aggressive expansion of the retail business, and the rollout of 5G services.

Analysts have responded positively to these announcements, with several upgrading their target prices for the stock. The company's diversified business model and strong execution capabilities continue to attract investor interest.

Key Highlights

  • Stock reached all-time high of 2,343.90
  • Zero-net debt target in 18 months
  • Aggressive retail expansion planned
  • 5G services rollout announced

Tata Motors to raise $1 billion for EV business

Positive 2 days ago 4 min read
RT
Reuters
Business News

Tata Motors has announced that it will raise $1 billion from private equity firm TPG for its electric vehicle (EV) subsidiary, valuing the EV business at $9.1 billion.

The investment comes as Tata Motors accelerates its push into electric vehicles in India, where it is already the market leader with models like the Nexon EV and Tigor EV.

The funds will be used to expand manufacturing capacity, develop new EV models, and strengthen the charging infrastructure network across India. The company aims to launch 10 new EV models by 2026.

Key Highlights

  • $1 billion funding from TPG
  • EV business valued at $9.1 billion
  • Market leader in India EV segment
  • Plans to launch 10 new EV models by 2026

Industry analysts view this as a positive development, noting that the capital infusion will help Tata Motors maintain its competitive edge in the rapidly growing EV market.
